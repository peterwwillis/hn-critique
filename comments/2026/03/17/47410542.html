wei03288  | rank #2  |  intelligent thoughtful
The discoverability problem for small sites is a distribution issue more than a quality issue. Most independent blogs produce genuinely useful content, but search engines have structurally deprioritized them in favor of sites with SEO budgets and link networks. What's interesting about Kagi's approach is that it doesn't try to fix the ranking algorithm — it just adds a separate signal. The question is whether that signal stays clean as it scales, or whether it becomes another thing people optimize for.
