Main Points & Arguments

  • Rust's coherence and orphan rules restrict trait implementations to prevent conflicts and ensure soundness, but they also hinder ecosystem evolution by making it difficult to replace foundational crates or introduce alternatives.
  • The orphan rules enforce that only traits or types defined in the current crate can have trait implementations, limiting downstream crates from implementing traits for foreign types.
